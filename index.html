The project

Diagnosis, without the wait for an expert

No lab, no waiting for a visit — just a phone camera and a clear answer, right where the crop is growing.

Patrika reads the same signs a plant pathologist looks for — leaf spotting, discoloration, edge curling, and pattern of spread — and turns them into a plain-language diagnosis in seconds.

Upload a photo of a leaf and it flags whether the plant is healthy, and if not, names the likely disease along with the treatment to start with. It won't replace an agronomist, but it will tell you when to call one.

Field notes

What it looks for

Three checks run on every photo, in this order.

01

Healthy check

Confirms uniform colour and intact leaf edges before looking for anything wrong.

02

Disease match

Compares spot shape, colour and spread pattern against known crop diseases.

03

Treatment note

Pairs the match with the treatment step to start with, and when to escalate.
